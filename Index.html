Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Procedure

  1. Mix flour, sugar & baking powder.
  2. Whisk in milk & egg until smooth.
  3. Pour ¼ cup batter on a hot pan; flip when bubbles form.

Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • Salt & pepper
  • 1 tbsp butter

Procedure

  1. Beat eggs with milk, salt & pepper.
  2. Heat butter, pour egg mix.
  3. Stir gently until soft curds form.

Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1 cup croutons
  • ½ cup Parmesan
  • ½ cup Caesar dressing

Procedure

  1. Toss lettuce with dressing.
  2. Add croutons & Parmesan.
  3. Serve immediately.

Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 slices cheddar
  • Butter

Procedure

  1. Butter bread exterior.
  2. Place cheese between slices.
  3. Grill until golden brown each side.

Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 4 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 cups vegetable broth

Procedure

  1. Sauté onion & garlic until soft.
  2. Add tomatoes & broth; simmer 20 min.
  3. Blend until smooth; season & serve.

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients

  • 200 g spaghetti
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • Red pepper flakes

Procedure

  1. Cook spaghetti al dente.
  2. Sauté garlic & flakes in oil.
  3. Toss pasta; season & serve.

Chicken Stir‑Fry

Ingredients

  • 200 g chicken breast
  • Mixed veggies
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil

Procedure

  1. Slice chicken & veggies.
  2. Heat oil; cook chicken until browned.
  3. Add veggies & soy; stir 5‑7 min.

Fruit Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup berries
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp honey

Procedure

  1. Combine all in blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour and enjoy chilled.

Veggie Omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cup peppers & onions
  • Salt & pepper
  • Butter

Procedure

  1. Beat eggs with salt & pepper.
  2. Sauté veggies.
  3. Pour eggs, cook until set.

Chocolate Mug Cake

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 1 tbsp oil

Procedure

  1. Mix all in a mug.
  2. Microwave 1–2 min until risen.
  3. Let rest briefly; serve warm.
